About:

Having a handheld console when we were little was extraordinary; playing pokemon and harvest moon just feels superb. Gone are the days when you could play all night then suddenly, your mother comes barging into your room to check if you were sound asleep. This developed a special skill to quickly hide your console under your pillow to avoid getting caught.



Origin:

This meme started in November 2019 when handheld consoles were being replaced by smartphones. Smartphones can pretty much perform what a handheld console does, so this meme emerged to remind us of the good old days.
