WATER PLEASE!

About:

In the game of ice-ice-water, the group must decide who is the ‘it’. Then the ‘it’ will try to ‘ice’ everyone. When you are iced by the ‘it’, you must not move until someone ‘waters’ you. We remember every thrilling moment that comes with waiting for someone to ‘water’ us.



Origin:

This meme originated in the Philippines since the game is widely played by kids in barrios and even narrow streets in the city. It’s just like a game of tag but there is a small twist within the game.
