COOKIE BOX

About:

Ever had a memory that you saw a cookie box but when you open it up there are sewing materials. Then, grandmother comes to the rescue and shows you a legit box of cookies that you dream of tasting ever since you saw that fake box of cookies.



Origin:

The cookie box meme circulated online when people had similar experiences of having a cookie box at home, only to find out that it contains sewing materials. You'd probably think that there’s some cookies in there but nope, it’s a sewing kit for our mothers.
