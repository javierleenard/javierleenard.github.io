About:

If you ever manage to see a street food vendor who puts their marinated food in a paint bucket then that is the secret ingredient why street foods tend to be delicious. You may find it weird to see that your food is inside of that bucket but still that is their way to utilize each item rather than throwing it away.



Origin:

This meme started during the first weeks of the pandemic where people are still going out then at some point they managed to encounter a vendor who is using a paint bucket as a tool for marinating containers of the food.
