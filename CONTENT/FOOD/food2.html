BUKO JUICE AQUARIUM

About:

Normally when a store is selling a drink especially in buko juices it is always stored in a jug or in a juice container but it's time to adapt for more juice this time the juice is stored in an aquarium instead of fish or any aquatic life.



Origin:

This meme started during the first months of the Pandemic where people are still opting in going out to their houses then all of a sudden there is one store that was able to utilize the aquarium rather than using it for fish display purposes. It was still safe though as long as there were no living fish inside the aquarium which was full of buko juices.
